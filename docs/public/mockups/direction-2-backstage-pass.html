Direction 2: Backstage Pass

Theatrical & Warm

This direction draws on the energy of being backstage at a live show. The user is the star getting ready to go on. Velvet curtain colors (deep burgundy, wine), gold marquee accents, playbill typography, and spotlight gradients create a world that feels personal and warm rather than clinical. Rest is the green room — a place of permission, not punishment. The show structure creates gentle urgency without anxiety: you are performing your day, not being managed through it.

Palette: wine (#a8304a) · gold (#d4a547) · cream (#f0e8d8) · stage dark (#1c1410) · curtain (#2a1018)
