Broadcast Control Room

The user is the showrunner of their own day, operating from a broadcast control room. Dark surfaces with focused, functional lighting. Monospaced countdown clocks. Amber "ON AIR" energy for active states. Every element earns its place on the stage manager's clipboard. Dense but warm — this is a comedy show's control room, not a space station.

Design tokens derived from the existing Showtime CLUI dark palette (#242422, #d97757, #ccc9c0). Category colors: Deep Work (purple), Exercise (green), Admin (blue), Creative (amber), Social (pink).
