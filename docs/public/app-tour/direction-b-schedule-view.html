Design Notes

👁

Dual-column drift

Left column shows the original plan (faded, dashed). Right column shows what actually happened (solid). The vertical offset between them IS the story — you can see at a glance how the day drifted. No judgment, just awareness.

🔴

NOW marker

Pulsing red line with tally dot. Auto-scrolls to keep the current moment in view. Everything above is history, everything below is forecast.

⭐

Beat stars on completed acts

Gold stars on actual blocks show where presence was locked. They are the show highlights. No stars on planned blocks — plans don't earn beats, showing up does.

🔌

Hover reveals the drift

Hovering any actual block shows a tooltip comparing planned vs actual times, with a drift summary. "+15 MIN" badges visible at a glance. On-time acts get a green badge.

☕

Intermissions breathe

Breaks appear as gentle gaps between blocks — warm, dashed borders, never guilt. They grow or shrink with the drift. Rest is free.

🔁

Toggle to Lineup view

The Schedule/Lineup toggle switches between this timeline view and the existing card-based lineup. Same data, different lens. Schedule is for awareness, Lineup is for action.
