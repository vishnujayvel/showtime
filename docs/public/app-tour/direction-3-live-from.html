Direction 3: Live From... — Cinematic Minimal

Takes the SNL energy without literal production references. Apple TV+ title cards, cinematic dark mode, bold typography that commands attention, generous whitespace. The user feels like they're starring in their own prestige show. One accent color (warm coral-amber) dominates a near-black canvas. Information density is deliberately low — only what matters right now. Rest costs zero.

5 — Beat Check Modal

Did you have a moment
of presence?

Were you here — even briefly — focused on what mattered?

Ultra-simple. The question is the entire screen. "Yes" is the obvious, easy action. "No" is small and carries zero judgment — it just moves on. The subtle accent glow makes the modal feel warm, not interrogative.

6 — Intermission

You've earned this pause.

Maximum calm. The screen is nearly empty — just one softly breathing line of text on the darkest surface. No timer, no stats, no guilt. Rest costs zero. The "tap to return" is so subtle it almost disappears.
