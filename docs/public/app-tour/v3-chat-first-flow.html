The planning phase is rebuilt. Everything after "WE'RE LIVE!" is the same.

💬

Chat Freely

No rigid Energy → Dump → Lineup steps. Talk to Claude about your day naturally. Ask questions, check your calendar, brainstorm. The lineup emerges from the conversation.

NOW: Free-form chat

Editable Lineup

The lineup appears as an inline card in the chat. Every field is clickable: name, duration, category. Drag to reorder. Or just tell Claude what to change. Both work.

Same Live Show

Everything after "WE'RE LIVE!" is identical. ON AIR light, countdown timer, beat checks, intermissions, strike the stage. The live show experience is proven and unchanged.

BEFORE: Same

Key Insight: Calendar Integration Becomes Natural

In the old flow, calendar events would need a dedicated step or import button. In the chat-first flow, the user just says "check my calendar" and Claude uses the Google Calendar tool. Calendar events appear inline in the conversation and get woven into the lineup naturally. The 📅 marker shows which acts came from the calendar, so the user always knows.

Note: Dual Edit Modes

The lineup card supports two editing modes simultaneously:

  • Direct manipulation — Click any field to edit it. Click the category badge to change it via dropdown. Drag to reorder.
  • Chat refinement — Type "make the run 45 minutes" or "swap acts 3 and 4" and Claude updates the lineup card in response.

Both approaches update the same lineup data. This gives ADHD brains the flexibility to use whichever mode feels right in the moment.
