Rationale

Why a Rundown Bar?

Broadcast DNA

TV producers use rundown sheets to track the live show. The horizontal timeline maps directly to the SNL metaphor — you see the whole show, where you are, and what is ahead.

Reality vs. Plan

When an act runs over, its block physically grows. Future acts compress. The bar is a living document — the plan bends to reality, not the other way around. No guilt, just truth.

Glanceable Progress

At 28-32px tall, the rundown bar adds show-wide context without stealing attention from the timer hero. The colored blocks and NOW marker tell the story in milliseconds.

ADHD-Friendly

Visual proportionality answers "how much is left?" without reading numbers. The overrun hatching pattern says "this ran long" without saying "you failed." Information without judgment.

Implementation Notes

  • • Block widths = actualDuration / totalShowDuration * 100% for completed acts, plannedDuration / totalShowDuration * 100% for future acts
  • • NOW marker position = (completedTime + currentActElapsed) / totalShowDuration * 100%
  • • Overrun hatching width = (actualDuration - plannedDuration) / actualDuration * 100% of that block
  • • All transitions use spring-like cubic-bezier cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) to match Framer Motion feel
  • • In Pill view, the rundown bar compresses to 4px height — category colors are sufficient at this scale, no labels needed
  • • Tooltips positioned with Radix UI Popover in production for collision-aware placement
